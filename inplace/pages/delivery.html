Our approach to methodologies is to always start with the challenge at hand to design a bespoke approach that best suits the need. We are qualitative experts who are genuinely excited by innovative methods. Our key principles are:

We Deliver…

Technology

Using technology to create seamless experiences and most effectively capture data

We seek and partner with the most innovative research software providers

We design approaches that use technologies that people are already familiar with and using everyday e.g. smartphones, go pros

We make use of data that is readily available to augment our findings e.g. open source data

Holistic

A holistic approach to the research process: while our expertise is in qualitative research, we understand quantitative research and are able to integrate both qual and quant into research planning and strategy. We have trusted partners that are able to execute complex quantitative phases

Creativity

We apply the same principles in presenting our findings with the goal of creating fun, engaging and truly impressive presentations, workshops, immersive experiences and/or exhibits

We endeavour to tell the story in the most concise and impressive way possible, using multimedia, immersion and inviting participation

We are able to collaboratively incorporate quantitative and data-driven approaches and impactful presentations such as data visualization

We proactively keep ourselves immersed in the latest artistic and technological developments e.g. AR, VR, maker workshops… and are able to design and collaboratively execute events and exhibitions

Creativity and warmth to best investigate and communicate the human experience

Some of our favorite methodologies include walking, in person observations, ethnographic interviews, sensory exploration
